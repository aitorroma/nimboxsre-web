What are cookies

A cookie is a small file that a website saves in the visitor's browser to store or retrieve information. They are commonly used to remember preferences, maintain sessions or measure audience.

Cookies used on this site

The site nimboxsre.com It is static and informative. The details of what it stores:

  • Consent Preference (strictly required): When you interact with the cookie banner, your choice is saved in your browser (through the consent library) so that it is not shown to you again on each visit. It does not identify the user nor is it shared with third parties.
  • Third party cookies: none. The site does not load external resources – the fonts and the consent library are served from the domain itself – nor does it integrate analytics, maps, embedded videos or social buttons.
  • Analytical or advertising cookies: none active. The banner includes an “Analytics” category planned for the future; As long as no tool is incorporated, nothing is executed even if it is accepted.

As the only information stored is the consent preference - strictly necessary - the site operates in accordance with article 22.2 of the LSSI-CE. You can review or change your choice at any time from the link «Cookie preferences» of the footer.
